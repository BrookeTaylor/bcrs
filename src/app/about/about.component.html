About Us

Our mission is clear: to provide fast, reliable, and cost-effective computer repair services while fostering a welcoming environment where customers can feel at ease. We believe that no computer problem is too big or too small, and we're dedicated to helping our clients navigate the ever-evolving world of technology.

Bob's Computer Repair Shop is more than just a business; it's a passion project brought to life by the visionary owner, Bob Lauori. Established in 1999, our shop has been a trusted destination for computer enthusiasts and everyday users alike, seeking expert assistance in diagnosing and resolving their computer woes. Bob Lauori's journey in the world of computers began years ago, driven by an insatiable curiosity and an innate knack for troubleshooting. His dedication to understanding the intricacies of computer systems led him to embark on this entrepreneurial venture, turning his passion into a thriving enterprise.

Bob's Computer Repair Shop isn't just a place to fix computers; it's a community of like-minded individuals who share a passion for all things tech. We continuously invest in learning and adapting to the latest advancements in the industry to better serve our customers. Our commitment to staying at the forefront of technology ensures that we can always offer the best solutions available.

As a new member of our team, you'll not only gain valuable technical experience but also become part of the Bob's Computer Repair Shop family. We encourage our employees to grow, learn, and innovate alongside us, just as Bob has done throughout his career.
